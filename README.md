

# Compute Central – Personal Tech Blog

Welcome to **Compute Central**, my personal technology blog where I share insights, learnings, and experiences from my journey in the tech world.

## About the Blog

This blog started back in **2016**, during my second year of pursuing a **Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)**. Initially, it was launched on **Google Blogger** under the name **EnhanceofIT**, driven by curiosity and a desire to document what I was learning.

Over time, the blog evolved:

* **EnhanceofIT (2016)** → My early experiments with blogging
* **Compute Central** → A refined space for structured and meaningful tech content

Today, Compute Central reflects my continued passion for writing and sharing knowledge.

## ✍️ Why I Write

I write because I genuinely enjoy it.
This blog is a space where I:

* Share technical concepts in a simple way
* Document personal learnings and experiences
* Explore ideas beyond day-to-day work

Most of my writing now happens **on weekends**, alongside my professional commitments.

## ⚠️ Note on Updates

Due to work deliverables and the rapidly evolving **AI-driven tech landscape**, updates may not always be frequent or fully up-to-date.

Also, with the shift toward newer content platforms, traditional blogging is less active—but this space remains important to me.
